# Contest

The Individual Contest and Team Contest are scheduled to take place at the *Faculty of Law* of the *University of Bucharest* (Facultatea de Drept, Universitatea București).

{{ image_row(category="venues-contest") }}

***The Faculty of Law*** is one of the oldest faculties of the University of Bucharest, having been recognized as an independent institution in 1859. Over many generations, the professors of the Faculty of Law have been involved in drafting the most important laws of Romania: the Romanian constitutions, the civil, criminal, civil procedure and criminal procedure codes, and have been permanently in the midst of the city's life, often exercising the highest positions in the state: presidents of the chambers of parliament, prime ministers, ministers and state secretaries.

## Park Inn by Radisson

{{ image_row(category="accommodation-park-inn") }}

***Park Inn by Radisson*** benefits from a central location in Bucharest, just 300 meters from the Romanian Athenaeum. From museums and galleries to churches and monuments, the center of Bucharest awaits you with a wealth of interesting sights. The hotel's location is a good starting point for exploring destinations such as Revolution Square, the lively Old Town and the National Village Museum. Cișmigiu Park, an oasis of greenery, is just a few steps from the hotel.

## Radisson Blu

{{ image_row(category="accommodation-radisson-blu") }}

Located in the heart of Bucharest, on the famous Calea Victoriei Boulevard, ***Radisson Blu Hotel, Bucharest*** stands out for its elegant appearance and modern design. The hotel is just 18 kilometers from the airport and 500 meters from the metro station, so you can easily walk around the city. Nearby you will also find the Romanian Athenaeum Concert Hall and the National Museum of Art.

# Transport

Delegations are expected to arrive either at ***Bucharest Henri Coandă International Airport (OTP)*** or the smaller ***Aurel Vlaicu Airport (BBU)***, from which bus/train transfer will be provided by the LOC. For attendees arriving at the ***Bucharest North railway station***, pick-up and transfer will be organized separetly. Other pick-up/transfer options might be possible, but not guaranteed, so please inform the LOC on any such special request.
