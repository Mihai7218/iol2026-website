# How to register

Registration will be done through the Registration System. A link will be provided here once it opens. Details about account creation will be provided before then.

## For non-accredited countries

> Countries which have not yet completed the accreditation process may be permitted to send one team to attend the Olympiad as guests at the discretion of the International Board in consultation with the Local Organisers. 

Excrept of article 2.3.4 of the [Regulations of the IOL](https://ioling.org/rules/rules.pdf).

## For visa-exempt countries/territories
The list of countries/territories that do not require a visa for stays of up to 90 days within any 180-day period, as well as specific conditions for each nationality, can be consulted [here](https://eviza.mae.ro/LongStayVisa).

The ETIAS travel authorisation is not expected to be implemented before late 2026. For up-to-date information, consult [this](https://travel-europe.europa.eu/etias) link.

## For visa-required countries/territories
Applications for a visa can be submitted online and sent to the appropriate diplomatic mission on the [Ministry of Foreign Affairs website](https://eviza.mae.ro/home). An application guide can be found [here](https://eviza.mae.ro/HowTo). Note that **each** applicant needs to submit their own visa application. Contact us for the required letters of invitation.
